Bamdam's Movie Recommendations.

My top 3 K-drama.

Mr Queen

This is the story of a man who travelled back in time to the Joseon Dynasty but he found himself in a woman's body.
It is purely comedy and I rate it 10/10.

Marry my Husband

This is the story of a woman who had a bad marriage but got a chance to go as far as 10 years back to correct the future. (I guess I wish to travel back every once or twice, because I love time travel movies too much).
I rate the movie 9.5/10, there were some unnecessary scenes, hence my ratings.

What's Wrong With Secretary Kim?

This is no time travel stuff, but the comedy is on point. Especially with the boss' emphasis on his "aura"(watch and understand, 'laughs in the witchly way')
I rate it 9/10. Although, it is meant to be a romance movie but I didn't get the full romAance vibes.
